The deployment of North Korean troops to Russia is driven by several factors. One primary reason is the strengthening of military ties between Pyongyang and Moscow. As both nations face increasing isolation from Western powers, they are looking to each other for support.

Motivations Behind Deployment:

  • Economic Support: North Korea is grappling with severe economic challenges. By aligning with Russia, it hopes to gain access to resources and financial aid.
  • Strategic Positioning: For Russia, having North Korean troops can help bolster its presence in Eastern Europe and counter Western influence.
